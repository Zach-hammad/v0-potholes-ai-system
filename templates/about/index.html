About POTHOLES

Advanced AI-powered pothole detection and management system for safer roads and efficient infrastructure maintenance.

Our Mission

POTHOLES is dedicated to revolutionizing road maintenance through cutting-edge artificial intelligence and community engagement. We believe that safer roads lead to stronger communities, and our technology makes it possible to identify, prioritize, and address road hazards more efficiently than ever before.

By combining AI-powered detection with citizen reporting and professional management tools, we create a comprehensive ecosystem for proactive infrastructure maintenance.
